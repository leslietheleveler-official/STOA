The Standard Theory of Action (STOA)

A cosmological, physical, and psychological framework for all past and future actions. This is based not on orthogonal causality, but tetrahedral: any vector can be defined from any point in space by calculating its relevance to four equal, positive-only, tetrahedral axes.

Thesis: The Universe's natural tendency is toward a state of predictable inertia and eventual entropy. Willful Agency is the sole emergent power capable of creating new and unpredictable Action. I have dubbed willful choice as "valkyric action" to differentiate it from deterministic and random action. Every action has a combinatory tetraction, a causal reaction along the positive, tetragonal axes of relative space. The STOA proposes the most fundamental unit of force is the information bit, and its force carrier is the photon, limited by the topology of our Universe. This is why photons have energy but no mass or dimensionality, and have a fundamental restriction on velocity in our Universe, which is C.

The STOA proposes that the Gluon Field is the local expression of a higher-dimensional Tetraction, not a force of simple conveyance.
